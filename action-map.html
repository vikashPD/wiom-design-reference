अभी करना है vs काम चल रहा है

Every state across all task flows — classified by what CSP must do and what's already happening. Detailed enough to make design decisions.

The simple rule: If the partner has a step pending, the task is Needs Action. If it's waiting on someone else or just an update, it's In Motion. Done / resolved tasks automatically leave the feed.

📋 Batch Card Behaviour · बैच कार्ड कैसे काम करता है

Recharge and Pickup tasks appear as grouped batch cards on the home feed — not individual cards. Here's how they work.

⚡ Recharge Batch Card

Multiple recharge collections grouped into one card. Shows count + individual rows with per-item CTA.

⚡ 4 Recharge collections due
Earliest: 2 hrs
Rajesh K. · Sector 12 · #4821
2h left
Meena S. · Lajpat Nagar · #3392
5h left
Amit P. · Dwarka · #7710
1d left
Sunita D. · Rohini · #5501
2d left
How it works:
  • ≤ 6 items: All rows visible inline — each row has its own "Collect" CTA
  • > 6 items: First 6 visible + "View All →" link at bottom that opens full list drilldown
  • Sorted by deadline — most urgent collection on top (orange/red deadline)
  • Per-item action: Tapping "Collect" on any row opens that specific customer's recharge flow
  • Card count updates in real-time as CSP completes collections
  • Card disappears from feed when all items are collected or expired
What CSP sees at a glance: "I have 4 recharge collections today, the most urgent one is Rajesh in 2 hours"

📦 NetBox Pickup Batch Card

Multiple device pickups grouped into one card. Shows count + individual rows with per-item status/CTA.

1d left
Karan S. · Vasant Kunj · NB-6654
3d left
Anita R. · GK-II · NB-2217
Verifying
How it works:
  • ≤ 6 items: All rows visible inline — each at its own state in the pickup flow
  • > 6 items: First 6 visible + "View All →" link for full drilldown
  • Mixed states in one card: Some rows say "Acknowledge", some say "Submit Photos", some show "Verifying" — each pickup is at a different step
  • Per-item action: Tapping a row's CTA opens that specific device's pickup flow (acknowledge → photos → verify)
  • Deadline sorting: Most urgent pickup on top, completed/verifying items pushed to bottom
  • Row disappears when that individual pickup is verified and resolved
  • Card disappears when all pickups are resolved
What CSP sees at a glance: "I have 3 pickups — Priya's is due tomorrow, Karan's I already collected but need photos, Anita's is being verified"

Batch Card vs Individual Card — when does what appear?

Task Type Card Style Why?
🏠 Install Individual card Each install is unique — different customer, location, slot, technician. Needs its own dedicated card + drilldown.
🔧 Service Individual card Each service issue is unique — different fault type, SLA, escalation path. Needs dedicated attention.
📦 Pickup Batch card Multiple pickups are similar — go to location, collect device, photograph. Grouping reduces feed clutter. CSP can plan a pickup route.
🚚 Shifting Individual card Each shifting involves unique addresses, coordination. Treated individually.
⚡ Recharge Batch card Multiple collections are repetitive — call customer, collect money, confirm. Grouping lets CSP see total pending + plan collection rounds.
✅
Done / Resolved — tasks auto-leave the feed. CSP doesn't need to dismiss them. Card count on home updates immediately.

Urgency System · कार्ड कब तेज़ चमकता है

How the app tells CSP that something is time-sensitive through visual signals
NORMAL Deadline is far away Grey/secondary text. No background tint. Card blends into feed normally.
URGENT Deadline approaching Orange warning background + orange deadline text + clock icon turns red. Card stands out.
OVERDUE Deadline passed Red negative background + bold "overdue" text. Card is visually loud. In Restore tasks, the entire card gets a red background tint.

Note: Urgency applies on top of both Needs Action and In Motion cards. An "In Motion" card can also become URGENT if the technician's deadline is approaching.
